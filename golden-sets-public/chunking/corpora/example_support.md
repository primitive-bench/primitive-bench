# Support Chat: Account Migration

Customer: Hi, I just upgraded to the Team plan and now half of my saved dashboards are missing from the sidebar. Can you help me get them back?

Agent: Sorry about that! When an account upgrades, dashboards owned by your personal workspace are not moved automatically — they stay in the old workspace until you transfer them. Nothing has been deleted.

Customer: That is a relief. How do I transfer them to the Team workspace?

Agent: Open Settings, then Workspaces, and click "Move content". Select the dashboards you want and choose the Team workspace as the destination. The move usually finishes within a minute, and shared links keep working afterward.

Customer: Done. They are showing up now. One more thing — my teammate cannot edit them even though I shared the workspace with her.

Agent: Sharing a workspace grants view access by default. To let her edit, open the workspace member list, find her name, and change her role from Viewer to Editor. Editors can create and modify dashboards but cannot delete the workspace itself.

Customer: Perfect, she can edit now. Thanks for the quick help!

Agent: Happy to help. As a tip, you can avoid the migration step next time by creating new dashboards directly inside the Team workspace. Have a great day!
